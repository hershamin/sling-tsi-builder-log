The rudder cables were routed the length of the rear fuselage, running from the baggage compartment area aft through the floor channels and bulkheads to the tail-cone. The cable support bracket was also fitted to the floor to guide the runs.

## Rudder Cable Routing
The cables run aft along the floor channels, passing through the bulkheads with their rod-end fittings.

## Cable Support Bracket
A boxed support bracket was mounted to the floor between the channels to guide and locate the cable runs.
