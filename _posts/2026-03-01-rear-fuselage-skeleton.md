Assembling the rear fuselage skeleton was straightforward, there were some hard to rivet places, but it was managed overall using the hand riveter. Additionally, the rear fuselage floor assembly was used along with the rib assemblies mentioned in the earlier fuselage posts.

## Skeleton
The overall rear fuselage skeleton involved the longerons as well as the rib sub-assemblies and the floor sub-assembly. Assembling was straightforward with some hard to reach places.
